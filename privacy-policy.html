Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Reto Born collects the following information:

  • Registration Information: Name and gender (to personalize workout routines)
  • Audio Data: Temporary voice recordings for the virtual assistant chat
  • Usage Information: Workout progress, app interactions
  • Contact Information: Email address (when using the contact form)
  • Subscription Information: Payment data processed securely through Apple

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your training experience based on gender and level
  • Provide voice chat service with Emilio Born
  • Save and display your progress in the program
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve our services and content

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using:

  • SSL-encrypted protected servers
  • Voice recordings are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after
  • We do not store credit card information (processed by Apple)
  • Industry-standard security measures to protect your information

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • OpenAI: To process voice commands (without identifiable information)
  • ElevenLabs: To generate assistant voice responses
  • Supabase: For secure data storage
  • Cloudflare: For video content delivery
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Reset your progress at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Reto Born is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The app uses minimal tracking technologies necessary to:

  • Keep your session active
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve app performance

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information while your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request account deletion at any time.
